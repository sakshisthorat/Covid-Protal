Criteria for Sellers

1. List medicine supplies related to covid-19 treatment (medicine, immunity boosters, etc.)
2. Enter your business details, product details and address details.
3. Complete your seller profile and buyers can see your profile.
4. Once your profile is created, buyers can contact you.

Criteria for Buyers

1. Tell us what you need by filling the form like type of medicines or immunity boosters required.
2. Receive Verified supplier details by location and your medicine requirement.
3. On the basis of your requirement, you will see medicine suppliers.
4. Once suppliers are upto your requirement, contact the seller.

About Us
