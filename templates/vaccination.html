VACCINATION GUIDELINES

registration guideline

  • Register on the Co-WIN Portal and schedule your vaccination appointment. www.cowin.gov.in

  • Once registered, notification and information about the vaccine session date and time will be shared with the beneficiary.

about the vaccine

  • Vaccines are available from Government and Private Health Facilities as notified, known as COVID Vaccination Centres (CVCs)

  • The time interval between two doses of the Covishield/Covaxin vaccine has been extended from four-six weeks to four-eight weeks.

pre-vaccination guideline

  • Donot take covid-vaccine if you had tested covid positive in the last recent days. Take the consultation of the Doctor in order to take the vaccines.

  • The health status before taking vaccine should be normal i.e. One should not have cold, cough,etc.

post-vaccination guideline

  • It is advisable to person with confirmed or suspected with COVID-19 infection to receive complete schedule of COVID-19 vaccine irrespective of past history of infection with COVID-19.

  • It is absolutely necessary that everyone who has received the COVID-19 vaccine should continue to follow the COVID-19 appropriate behaviour.
