Question

1. Where can I register for COVID-19 vaccination?

Answer

You can log into the Co-WIN portal using the link www.cowin.gov.in and click on the “Register/Sign In Yourself” tab to register for COVID-19 vaccination.

Question

2. Is there a mobile app that needs to be installed
to register for vaccination?

Answer

There is no authorised mobile app for registering for vaccination in India. You need to log into the Co-WIN portal. You can also register for vaccination through the ArogyaSetu App.

Question

3. Which age groups can register for vaccination
on the Co-WIN portal?

Answer

At present all citizens above 60 years of age and those between the age of 45 and a 59 years with one of the specified comorbidities can register for vaccination.

Question

4. Is online registration mandatory for Covid 19
vaccination?

Answer

No. Vaccination Centres also provide for a limited number of on-spot registration slots every day. However, it is recommended to register online and schedule vaccination in advance for a hassle-free vaccination experience.

Question

5. How many people can be registered
in Co-WIN portal through one mobile number?

Answer

Up to 4 people can be registered for vaccination using the same mobile number.

Question

6. How can citizens manage online registration
without any source?

Answer

Up to 4 people can be registered for vaccination using the same mobile number. Citizens can take help from friends or family for online registration. Your own feature phone can also be used to register through a smart phone of your friend or registration on the Portal.

Question

7. Can I book an appointment for vaccination in
the Co-WIN portal?

Answer

Yes, you can book appointment for vaccination through Co-WIN portal after logging-in in the Co-WIN Portal through your registered mobile number.

Question

8. Can I download appointment slip apropriatly?

Answer

You can search in Co-WIN portal (or Aarogya Setu) for the vaccination centre nearest to your location by either searching through PIN code or by choosing the State and the District.

Question

9. How can I find the nearest vaccination centre?

Answer
You can search in Co-WIN portal (or Aarogya Setu) for the vaccination centre nearest to your location by either searching through PIN code or by choosing the State and the District.

Question

10. What if I cannot go for vaccination on the date of
appointment? Can I reschedule my appointment?

Answer

The appointment can be rescheduled at any time. In case you do not go for vaccination on the date of appointment, you can reschedule the appointment by clicking on “Reschedule” tab.

Question

11. Do I have an option for cancellation of the appoint-
ment?

Answer

Yes, you can cancel an appointment already scheduled. You can also reschedule the appointment and choose another date or time slot of your convenience.

Question

12. Where will I receive confirmation of date and time
of vaccination?

Answer

Once an appointment is scheduled, you will receive the details of the vaccination centre, date and time slot chosen for appointment in an SMS sent to your registered mobile number. You can also download the appointment slip and print it or keep it on your smart phone.

Question

13. Is it necessary to take 2nd dose of the vaccination?

Answer

Yes. It is recommended that both doses of vaccine should be taken for realising the full benefit of vaccination. Both doses must be of the same vaccine type.

Question

14. When should I take the 2nd dose of vaccination?

Answer

It is recommended that the 2nd dose of COVAXIN should be administered in the interval of 4 to 6 weeks from the date of 1st dose administration. For COVISHIELD the recommended interval is 4 to 8 weeks. You may choose the date of 2nd dose vaccination as per your convenience.
